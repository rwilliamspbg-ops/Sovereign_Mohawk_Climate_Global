# GENESIS_MANIFESTO

Sovereign Mohawk Climate Global is a commitment to sovereignty-first climate intelligence.

## Principles

- Sovereignty first: no raw environmental data leaves local custody by default.
- Collaboration without centralization: global climate model quality from local intelligence.
- Verifiable trust: attestation, proofs, and policy signatures are mandatory.
- Compliance by design: governance and data controls are built into the runtime.
- Resilience for all: from community sensors to national meteorological systems.
